Description of the special session:
The integration of emerging tools and techniques in healthcare is revolutionizing medical diagnostics, patient monitoring, and treatment planning. This special session aims to provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to present their latest findings in biomedical image processing, biomedical signal processing, wearable healthcare technologies, and the application of AI, machine learning, and deep learning in healthcare. Recent advancements in artificial intelligence and deep learning have significantly enhanced healthcare applications, enabling more accurate diagnostics, predictive analytics, and personalized treatments. The proposed session will highlight novel methodologies, algorithms, and real-world applications that contribute to improving patient outcomes and healthcare efficiency


Scope of the Special Session
• Biomedical image processing and segmentation.
• Biomedical signal processing and analysis.
• AI-driven diagnostics and healthcare solutions.
• Wearable healthcare monitoring and rehabilitation technologies.
• Machine learning for disease prediction and risk assessment.
• Deep learning applications in medical imaging and bioinformatics.
• Internet of Things (IoT) and smart healthcare systems.
